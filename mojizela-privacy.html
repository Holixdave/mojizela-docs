How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate, improve, and protect the Mojizela platform. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

  • Account management — Creating and authenticating your account, verifying your email, and managing session state via Firebase Auth.
  • Content delivery — Serving your feed (For You and Following), uploading and delivering media via Supabase CDN, and rendering posts.
  • Messaging — Transmitting and caching messages between users. Messages are stored in Firestore and cached locally on your device via SQLite.
  • Notifications — Sending push notifications for likes, comments, follows, messages, and call alerts via Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM).
  • Live streaming — Establishing and managing real-time audio/video channels via Agora RTC during live sessions.
  • AI assistant — Processing your messages with our AI backend to generate responses. Conversation history is stored locally on your device only.
  • Feed personalisation — Using engagement signals (likes, views, shares, watch time) to rank the For You feed algorithm.
  • Safety enforcement — Detecting violations of community guidelines and applying shadow bans or account suspensions where necessary.
  • Analytics and improvement — Understanding aggregate usage patterns to improve app performance, stability, and feature development.
🚫 We do not serve advertisements. Mojizela is entirely ad-free. Your data is never sold to advertisers or used for ad targeting.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data. We honour these requests for all users regardless of jurisdiction.

  • Access — You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — You may update your username, email, profile photo, and bio directly in the app at any time.
  • Deletion — You may request full deletion of your account and associated data. Email us at mojizela.softstudio@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my account". We will process the request within 30 days.
  • Data portability — You may request an export of your posts, profile data, and message history.
  • Object to processing — You may object to certain uses of your data, such as feed personalisation based on engagement signals.
  • Withdraw consent — Where processing is based on consent (e.g. push notifications), you may withdraw it at any time via device settings.
  • Restrict processing — In certain circumstances you may request that we limit how we process your data while a dispute is resolved.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at mojizela.softstudio@gmail.com. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

⚡ GDPR (EU/EEA users): You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority if you believe your data is being processed unlawfully. CCPA (California users): You have the right to know what personal information is collected, to delete it, and to opt out of its sale (we do not sell personal information).

Children's Privacy

Mojizela is intended for users aged 13 and older. In jurisdictions where a higher age of digital consent applies (e.g. 16 in some EU countries), users must meet the locally applicable minimum age.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us immediately at mojizela.softstudio@gmail.com and we will delete the account and all associated data without delay.

Parents or guardians who discover that their child has provided us with personal information without consent should contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Cookies & Tracking

Mojizela is a native mobile application and does not use browser cookies. On the app level, we use the following local storage mechanisms:

  • SharedPreferences — Stores app settings, theme preferences, and the intro-screen "seen" flag. Entirely local to your device.
  • Hive — Stores AI chat history and chat message cache. Entirely local, never synced to our servers.
  • SQLite (LocalDB) — Caches recent chat messages for offline access. Stored only on your device.
  • Firebase Auth token — A JWT token stored securely on device to maintain your logged-in session. This token is used only to authenticate requests to Firestore and is never shared with third-party advertisers.

Mojizela does not use cross-app tracking, advertising IDs for ad targeting, or third-party analytics SDKs beyond what is listed in the Third-Party Services section.

International Transfers

Mojizela is a global service. Your data may be processed in countries other than your own, including the United States, where our third-party providers (Firebase/Google, Supabase/AWS) maintain their primary infrastructure.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, these transfers are made in compliance with applicable data protection laws. Firebase (Google) participates in the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework. Supabase transfers data under Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).

🌍 By using Mojizela from outside the United States, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States and other countries where our service providers operate, which may have different data protection laws than your country.
